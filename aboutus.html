About Us

Hello everyone. Tired of frequently checking the date of your upcoming tests, quiz? Do you forget the deadline for assignment submission? Do you want the link for upcoming classes at the same place? Then, we have a good news for you. This website will pre-inform you the date of upcoming tests, deadline for assignment submission, and so on. The class representative must keep on updating the class join links or dates on this website and we shall keep on reminding you about all those things so that you don't miss any class or any test/ assignment.
Our Mission
Well, our mission is to make your life easy by frequently giving reminders related to your class timing, pending assignments, upcoming tests and quizzes. Now you don't have to text or call your friends to ask the joining link for classes,you don't have to remember the deadline for submitting your assignments. Wish you all the best for this online semester.
